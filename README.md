
*Install Love2d, a compact Lua interpreter.

*Go to https://love2d.org/ and download the MacOs 64 bit installer.

*You should get a security warning saying the application can not be verified.  Click on the question mark in the upper right hand corner of the pop-up.

*In the help text, click on the 'take me to security and privacy settings.'

*In the 'security section' you should see 'Love was recently blocked to protect your mac' and the option to 'Open Anyway.'  Click 'Open Anyway.'

*Now try and open the Love2d interpreter and the option to 'Open Anyway' should appear and prompt you to enter the user password.

*If the base project opens, you now have a Love and Lua interpeter on your machine.  

*Rename the root folder app.love, the .love extension should be recognized as a Love Application.  

*The icon should change to a heart icon.

*Right click on the application and under the 'Open With' options, select Love2D.
